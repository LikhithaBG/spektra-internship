Company culture refers to the set of values, ethics, and beliefs that define the day-to-day operations and atmosphere at an organization. It impacts everything from high-level business decisions to the vibe in the communal kitchen. Culture at an organization originates from leadership, but all levels within a company—from hiring to setting employment policies to interacting within a physical space—play a role in defining it
